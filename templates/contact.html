Before You Contact Me

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. Portrait sessions can usually be scheduled within 2-4 weeks.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! I require a 50% deposit to secure your date, with the balance due on your session day. Custom payment plans available for weddings.

Do you travel for sessions?

Absolutely! I love exploring new locations. Travel within 50 miles is included, with reasonable fees for destinations beyond that.

When will I receive my photos?

You'll get a sneak peek within 24 hours! Full galleries are delivered within 2-6 weeks depending on the session type.
