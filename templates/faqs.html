Sod Installation FAQs

We recommend different grass types based on your local climate, soil conditions, and intended use. For cooler climates, we suggest Kentucky Bluegrass or Fescue blends. For warmer areas, Bermuda or Zoysia grass work best. During our free consultation, we'll assess your property and recommend the perfect grass type for your specific needs.

Most residential sod installations are completed in 1-2 days, depending on the size of the area. This includes site preparation, soil amendments, and sod installation. Larger commercial projects may take 3-5 days. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during your consultation.

The best time for sod installation is during the growing season - typically spring (April-May) or early fall (September-October). These periods provide optimal temperatures and rainfall for root establishment. However, we can install sod year-round with proper care and irrigation.

New sod requires daily watering for the first 2-3 weeks, keeping the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged. Avoid foot traffic for the first 2 weeks. After 3-4 weeks, you can begin normal lawn care activities. We provide detailed care instructions and follow-up support for all installations.

Lawn Care & Maintenance FAQs

During the growing season, most lawns should be mowed weekly. However, this can vary based on grass type, weather conditions, and fertilization schedule. Cool-season grasses may need more frequent mowing in spring and fall, while warm-season grasses grow most actively in summer.

Our packages include regular mowing, edging, blowing off walkways, fertilization, weed control, and seasonal services. Premium packages also include aeration, overseeding, pest management, and priority scheduling. We customize each package to meet your specific lawn care needs.
